job at Tesla

If I had the choice to work at whatever company, I think I would go for the job as an software engineer for Tesla. This is because I like the cars and the technology that they create.

Tesla is a car company that creates electric cars, trucks, sunroofs and energystorages. It was founded in 2003 and owned by Elon Musk. The main focus of the company lays on the electric cars that have a lot of software on them so they can drive themselves and do a lot of other things.

Some information about the company:

  • 110.000 employees
  • 200 software engineers working on autopilot
  • They mostly make use of C#
  • They have 6 manufacturing facilities
  • Their headquarter is in Texas
