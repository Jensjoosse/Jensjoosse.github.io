My study choice

I have always been interested in experimenting with tech and trying new things out, but when I found video's about software engineering it really caught my attention so I started to watch more video's about it. Last year we had to attend at two informations days for a school project. So I directly signed up for two ICT programs, one of them was in Eindhoven at Fontys and the other one was at the University of Apllied sciences in Middelburg. After attending both of them I started compairing them and came to the conclusion that the University of Apllied Sciences was a smarter plan, beacause now I can live at my parents house, and otherwise I would have to go on rooms in Eindhoven and that would be a uneccesary expense.
Some of my strengths are that I am really curious and always want to learn new things, these do only work if I am interested in the subject. one of my weaknesses is that I am not the best at planning. This may hold me back and cause me to having to work late nights on projects.
In my prior education I have had a some programming lessons, these were mostly about HTML, CSS and C#, but I do not remember a lot of them.
When programming, there are a lot of times were I tend to get stuck. On these moments I really have to tell myself to stay calm and think about what logic sollutions are for the problems.
I will also have to tell myself this after my study, because I will try to find a job as an Software Engineer.
